Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 20, 2025

InstaAudit ("we", "our", or "us") provides an analytics service that helps users gain insights into their Instagram account performance. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use InstaAudit and authorize us via Meta’s Graph API, we may collect the following information from your Instagram account to perform a personalized audit:

  • Public Profile Information: Your username, profile picture, and account type (e.g., business or creator)
  • Bio and Website: The bio text and any website link included in your Instagram profile
  • Captions from Posts: The text you write under your posts (captions), to assess content tone, frequency, and engagement strategies
  • Engagement Metrics: Likes, comments, shares, saves, and engagement rate across your posts
  • Follower and Following Counts: To analyze audience growth and interaction potential
  • Content Statistics: Reach, impressions, profile visits, and other performance indicators available through Instagram Graph API
  • Post Timing and Frequency: We analyze when and how often you post
  • Other Authorized Data: Any other data permitted through your API authorization that helps improve your audit report

Note: We do not collect direct messages, login credentials, or any private/sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate an audit report on your Instagram performance
  • To provide personalized insights and recommendations
  • To improve our services and user experience

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers or third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is processed securely and only for the duration necessary to generate your report. We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties unless required by law or explicitly authorized by you.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You can request data deletion at any time by contacting us at your@email.com. We will delete your data from our systems within 7 business days.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the Meta Graph API to access Instagram data. Your use of Meta services is subject to their Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
